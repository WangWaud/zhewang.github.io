About Me关于我

I am a Ph.D. student in the Kenichi Tsuda lab at Huazhong Agricultural University. My research focuses on plant-pathogen-microbiome interactions, especially how phyllosphere microbes influence plant health, pathogen behavior, and microbiome-mediated disease outcomes.我目前是华中农业大学津田研一实验室的博士研究生。我的研究聚焦于植物–病原–微生物组互作，尤其关注叶际微生物如何影响植物健康、病原行为以及微生物组介导的病害结果。

I am particularly interested in combining multi-omics, microbial ecology, and experimental approaches to understand how plants recruit, shape, or depend on microbial communities under pathogen pressure. I also share research notes and scientific commentary through my WeChat Official Account.我尤其关注结合多组学、微生物生态学与实验方法，理解植物在病原压力下如何招募、塑造或依赖微生物群落。我也会通过微信公众号分享科研笔记与学术评论。
