About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Kenichi Tsuda lab at Huazhong Agricultural University. My research focuses on plant-pathogen-microbiome interactions, especially how phyllosphere microbes influence plant health, pathogen behavior, and microbiome-mediated disease outcomes.

I am particularly interested in combining multi-omics, microbial ecology, and experimental approaches to understand how plants recruit, shape, or depend on microbial communities under pathogen pressure. I also share research notes and scientific commentary through my WeChat Official Account.
